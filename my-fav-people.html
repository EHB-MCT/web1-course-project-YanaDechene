My Favorite People

My lover, Max

Max is my boyfriend, and we’ve been together for six months now. We first met at school in our makerslab course, both of us acting like complete idiots—and somehow, that’s exactly what made everything click. From that very first day, our personalities just fit effortlessly. What started as laughter and chaos slowly turned into something deeper, and before I knew it, we had become inseparable. Over time, those moments together turned into real feelings, and now he’s someone I genuinely can’t imagine my life without.

My bestfriend and soul brother, Zeno

Zeno has been part of my life for as long as I can remember—we’ve known each other since we were four. We grew up side by side, going to the same school for years, sharing so many memories that it feels like he’s family. He’s like a brother to me, someone I can talk to about absolutely anything without ever feeling judged. Some of my favorite moments with him are the random little “side-quests” we end up on, the kind that turn into the best stories later. Even though college has made it harder to see each other as often, that hasn’t changed a thing—he still is, and always will be, my best friend.
