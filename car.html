Cars

So you’re probably wondering why I, as a girl, am into cars—and honestly, I don’t fully have an answer for that. It just kind of happened. Over time, my social media feed started showing me more and more cars, and somewhere along the way, I realized how fascinating they actually are. There’s something about them that just pulls me in—the aggressive body kits, the raw sound of engines, the way each car has its own personality. And yes, I’m still a girl at heart, so the design and colors are easily my favorite part. That mix of power and aesthetics? That’s what makes me love them.
